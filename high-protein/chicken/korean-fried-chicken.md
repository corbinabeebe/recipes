### **ðŸ“ Ingredients (4 Servings)**

#### **For the Chicken**

* 700g **Chicken Breast** (Cubed)
* 2 tsp **Garlic Powder**
* 1.5 tsp **Black Pepper**
* 2 tsp **Paprika**
* 1 tsp **Salt**
* 30g **Cornflour** (or Corn Starch)

#### **For the Sticky Glaze**

* 50ml **Light Soy Sauce** mixed with 50ml **Water** (100ml total)
* 25g **Honey**
* 40g **Gochujang Paste** (Korean chili paste)
    * *Substitution:* Mix Tomato Paste with Sriracha if Gochujang is unavailable.

#### **For the Pickled Cucumbers (Optional)**

* Thinly sliced **Cucumber**
* 20ml **Light Soy Sauce**
* 10ml **Rice Vinegar**
* 1 tsp **Garlic Powder**
* 1 tsp **Sesame Seeds**
* **OPTIONAL:** Chilli Flakes (Skip if you prefer less spice)

---

### **ðŸ§‘â€ðŸ³ Step-by-Step Instructions**

1.  **Season the Chicken:** Place the cubed chicken breast in a bowl. Add the garlic powder, black pepper, paprika, and salt. Mix thoroughly.
2.  **Coat the Chicken:** Add the **30g of cornflour** to the seasoned chicken. Ensure the chicken pieces are **well-coated**.
    > *Note: You can skip the cornflour entirely if you do not have it.*
3.  **Cook the Chicken:** Cook the coated chicken in a hot pan (using a touch of oil or cooking spray) until it is cooked through and crispy on the outside.
4.  **Make the Glaze (IMPORTANT):**
    * In a separate bowl, combine all glaze ingredients: the soy sauce/water mixture, honey, and Gochujang paste.
    * Pour the glaze into a clean pan on **low heat**. **Keep stirring constantly** until the sauce starts to bubble lightly.
    * Allow the sauce to **reduce and thicken for 3-4 minutes**.
5.  **Glaze the Chicken:** Add the crispy chicken pieces to the thickened glaze. Toss gently to ensure every piece is thoroughly coated in the sticky sauce.
6.  **Prepare Cucumbers:** If making the pickled cucumbers, mix all cucumber ingredients together in a separate bowl.
7.  **Serve & Meal Prep:** Serve the Sticky Korean Fried Chicken over hot rice.

### **ðŸ“Œ Important Notes for Meal Prep**

* **Cucumbers:** It is best to **store the pickled cucumbers separately** and serve them cold. Do not warm them up with the rest of the meal prep.
* **Glaze:** Be sure to cook the glaze on low heat and stir continuously to prevent burning and ensure proper thickening.
